TIMP Contributors

TIMP has its origins in more than 15 years of software development for applications in computational physics by Ivo van Stokkum.

The primary developers are currently

  • Ivo van Stokkum
  • Katharine Mullen

Sergey Laptenok has contributed options for FLIM data and invaluable experience and testing on the MS Windows OS. Joris Snellenburg is currently investigating options for a cross-platform java-based GUI for TIMP. Alessandro Marin has tested much of the code and contributed many ideas for new options.

The members of the biophysics group of the Department of Physics and Astronomy of the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam have motivated much of TIMP's development.
